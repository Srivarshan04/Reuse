"Tamil Nadu & SDG-14: Guardians of the Blue Frontier"?

Sustainable Development Goal 14 (SDG-14) aims to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development. Tamil Nadu, with its vast coastline and rich marine biodiversity, plays a crucial role in India's commitment to this goal. However, it faces numerous challenges, including marine pollution, habitat destruction, overfishing, and climate change impacts.


This website highlights Tamil Nadu's efforts, challenges, and the steps taken to protect marine ecosystems.
